How to play:

Welcome to the "Whack-a-Mole" game where you can play as Will Smith and slap Chris Rock! Your mission is to whack Chris as he pops up from different holes using your mouse or touch screen. Keep an eye on the timer and try to beat your high score. With fast-paced action and hilarious sound effects, this game is sure to keep you entertained for hours.
